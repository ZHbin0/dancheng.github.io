Introduction

About Me

I am a radiologist and postdoctoral scholar at the University of Alabama at Birmingham, specializing in neuroradiology and vascular imaging. My research focuses on advancing neuroimaging techniques for improved diagnosis and understanding of cerebrovascular diseases and brain tumors.

With clinical training from both China and the United States, I bring a unique perspective to translational research in radiology. I am particularly interested in developing and applying novel imaging methods to improve patient outcomes in neurological conditions.
